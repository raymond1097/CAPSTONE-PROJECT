The Future of JS

JavaScript is evolving beyond the browser — powering servers, mobile apps, and AI-driven tools. With faster runtimes like Bun and Deno, and frameworks such as React and Next.js, development is becoming faster and smarter. The future of JavaScript is all about speed, simplicity, and innovation.
